# Optimizers

The following optimizers are supported:
- **torch**:
  - `Adam`
  - `AdamW`
- **bitsandbytes**:
  - `Adam`
  - `AdamW`
  - `Adam8Bit`
  - `AdamW8Bit`

> [!NOTE]
> Not all optimizers have been tested with all models/parallel settings. They may or may not work, but this will gradually improve over time.
